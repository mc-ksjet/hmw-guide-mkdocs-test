# Operator perks

See [Active Abilities](../active-abilities.md) for details of most of the perks
that Operator Officers have. Perks that are not Active Abilities are covered
below.

## Escort Ship: Escort Armour Damage per Hit

Increases armour damage per hit from escorts.

Abbreviation: **DMG-A**

| Officer Rarity by Level | 1    | 10   | 20   | 30   | 40   |
|-------------------------|:----:|:----:|:----:|:----:|:----:|
|Common        |+4%    |+7%     |+10%    |+13%    |+16%    |
|Uncommon      |+8%    |+11%    |+14%    |+17%    |+20%    |
|Rare          |+12%   |+15%    |+18%    |+21%    |+24%    |
|Epic          |+16%   |+19%    |+22%    |+25%    |+28%    |
|Legendary     |+24%   |+27%    |+30%    |+33%    |+36%    |
